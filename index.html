Frequently Asked Questions

From pet types to adoption and delivery services, find everything you need to know about our shop here!

What types of pets do you sell?

We sell a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and small animals like hamsters and guinea pigs. We ensure all our pets are well cared for and ready for loving homes.

Do you offer pet accessories and grooming products?

Yes! We have a wide range of pet accessories, including beds, toys, collars, leashes, and feeding bowls. We also offer grooming essentials like shampoos, brushes, and nail clippers to keep your pet looking and feeling great.

Do you provide home delivery for pet food and supplies?

Yes, we offer convenient home delivery for pet food, toys, and accessories in selected locations. Simply place your order online or visit our store to arrange delivery straight to your doorstep.

Do you offer veterinary services or pet health check-ups?

While we don’t have an in-house vet, we can recommend trusted veterinary clinics for health check-ups, vaccinations, and medical care. Feel free to ask us for referrals!

Do you have a pet adoption service?

Yes! We have a pet adoption program that helps animals find their forever homes. If you're looking to adopt, we can guide you through the process and help you find the perfect pet for your family.

Are the pets vaccinated before being sold?

Absolutely! All our pets receive necessary vaccinations before being sold. We also provide their health records so you can stay informed about their medical history and future care.

What is your return or exchange policy for pet products?

We accept returns or exchanges on unused products within 7 days of purchase, as long as you have the receipt. For hygiene reasons, opened food and grooming items cannot be returned. If you receive a defective product, we’ll gladly replace it.

What are your store hours and locations?

Our store is open Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 8 PM. You can visit us at our physical location or check our website for store details and contact information.
